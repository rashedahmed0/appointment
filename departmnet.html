Our Medical Departments

We offer comprehensive medical care through our specialized departments, each equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by expert healthcare professionals.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care with advanced diagnostic and treatment options for all cardiac conditions.

  • Cardiac Catheterization
  • Echocardiography
  • Heart Surgery
  • Pacemaker Implantation

Neurology

Expert care for disorders of the nervous system, including stroke, epilepsy, and neurodegenerative diseases.

  • EEG & EMG Testing
  • Stroke Treatment
  • Epilepsy Management
  • Neurological Rehabilitation

Emergency Medicine

24/7 emergency care for critical conditions with rapid response and advanced life support.

  • Trauma Care
  • Critical Care
  • Emergency Surgery
  • Acute Pain Management

Orthopedics

Treatment for musculoskeletal conditions, including joint replacement, sports injuries, and fractures.

  • Joint Replacement
  • Arthroscopic Surgery
  • Sports Medicine
  • Fracture Treatment

Pediatrics

Comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents in a child-friendly environment.

  • Vaccination Programs
  • Growth Monitoring
  • Childhood Disease Treatment
  • Developmental Assessment

General Surgery

Advanced surgical procedures using minimally invasive techniques for better patient outcomes.

  • Laparoscopic Surgery
  • Gastrointestinal Surgery
  • Endoscopic Procedures
  • Surgical Oncology
